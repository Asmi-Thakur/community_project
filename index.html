Wet Waste & Composting Hub

Managed by the Goregaon Green Warriors Group. We teach households how to turn kitchen waste into nutrient-rich soil!

Why Compost Kitchen Waste?

Over 60% of Mumbai's household garbage is wet organic waste. Composting at home drastically reduces landfill strain and cuts down toxic methane emissions.

Quick Composting Guide:

  • 🌱 Step 1: Separate cooked/raw kitchen waste from plastics.
  • 📦 Step 2: Add your waste to a ventilated bin layered with dry leaves or cocopeat.
  • 🔄 Step 3: Stir weekly. Beautiful, nutrient-rich soil is ready in 4–6 weeks!
